### **Project Description:**

The **Recipe Book & Meal Planner** is a web/mobile application designed to help users discover, save, and organize recipes while efficiently planning their meals. It combines the convenience of a digital recipe collection with intelligent meal planning features that promote healthy eating habits, reduce food waste, and simplify grocery shopping.

Users can browse a variety of recipes, customize meal plans based on dietary preferences, and automatically generate shopping lists based on selected meals. The app can also integrate nutritional tracking, ensuring users maintain balanced diets according to their health goals.
